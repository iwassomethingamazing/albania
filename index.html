Historical Timeline

2000 BC: Illyrian tribes establish settlements in the region.
1443: Skanderbeg leads a rebellion against the Ottoman Empire.
1912: Albania declares independence from the Ottoman Empire.
1991: The communist regime falls, and democracy begins.
